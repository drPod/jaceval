## Why this is the correct "LLMDocs" artifact for this eval

The jaceval design-recipe (see `design-recipe.md` §41) referenced Jaseci's "LLMDocs-Mini and LLMDocs-Full" variants. Investigation on 2026-04-19 showed that only a single artifact exists: `jac-llmdocs.md`, the "Jac Language Reference" auto-generated by the `jaseci-llmdocs` pipeline from the canonical docs. Earlier releases (v0.10.x) called it `candidate.txt`; from v0.12.0 onward it is `jac-llmdocs.md`. There is no separate Mini/Full split.

The spec's original 5-arm plan collapses to 4 arms: `no-skill`, `llmdocs`, `v0-skill`, `irrelevant-ctrl`. See spec revision note in §5.1.
